# 🚀 Beta Tester Agreement  
**Effective Date:** April 30, 2025  
**Applies To:** Users participating in early testing of *Star Citizen Kill Tracker*

Thank you for participating in the beta testing phase of the **Star Citizen Kill Tracker** ("the Tool"). By using a beta version, you acknowledge and agree to the following:

---

## 1. 🧪 Pre-Release Disclaimer

- This version is **in development** and may contain bugs, incomplete features, or unstable behavior.
- Features and functionality are **subject to change** without notice during the testing phase.

---

## 2. 🔐 Data & Privacy

- The Tool follows the same data handling policies outlined in the [Privacy Policy](#).
- No personal data is collected, and nothing is stored locally or remotely.
- All beta testers are expected to **report any bugs or suspicious behavior**.

---

## 3. 🛠️ Feedback & Reporting

- Please report bugs, crashes, or suggestions in the official [Support Discord](https://discord.gg/jxfHnGQqj7) or GitHub (if available).
- When reporting issues, include:
  - A brief description of the bug
  - Steps to reproduce (if possible)
  - Any relevant log output or screenshots

---

## 4. 🚫 Confidentiality

- While not under NDA, beta features should not be redistributed, reverse-engineered, or leaked publicly without prior approval.
- Screenshots, videos, and discussions about unreleased features should stay within official testing channels.

---

## 5. 📦 Leaving the Beta

- You can stop testing at any time. Simply remove the Tool from your system and discontinue use.
- Continued use of the beta after updates implies agreement to any revised terms.

---

> **We appreciate your help in shaping the future of the Star Citizen Kill Tracker. Your feedback makes this better for everyone.**
